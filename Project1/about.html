The Boys

Kendall Doll


Kendall Doll is a 26 year old theater-kid turned rock star. He went to Julliard for musical theater, but his passion is playing in this cover band he created with college friend Logan. Kendall's favorite song to cover is Til I Forget About You

James Ames


James Ames is more of a math nerd than a rock star. He went to Harvard and majored in Mathematics. One day a friend from high school reached out and asked if James could play the drums for his cover band and, well, the rest is history... James' favorite song to cover is Worldwide.

Carlos Arlos


Carlos Arlos played hockey for NYU, and always thought he'd go pro. But after listening to a group of guys performing in a bar one night, he felt the urge to join them. Carlos Arlos' favorite song to cover is Windows Down.

Logan Hogan


Logan Hogan is a New York local, and met Kendall at a bar one night where they did karaoke. They formed this cover band as a joke, but then realized they were pretty good... Logan's favorite song to cover is Big Time Rush.
